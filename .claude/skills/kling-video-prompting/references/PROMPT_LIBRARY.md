# Kling AI Prompt Library — Production Templates

Tested prompts organized by production category. All optimized for Kling 2.5–3.0, Professional mode, 16:9, 5–10 seconds.

Each template follows the formula: **Subject + Action → Camera → Environment + Lighting → Style**

---

## 1. Cinematic narrative

### Detective reveal (slow pan)
```
The camera pans slowly to the right, moving away from the detective's shocked face, who remains petrified. The pan gradually reveals a massive clue board on the wall, covered in photos, maps, and red string connecting clues. Cinematic, suspenseful, dramatic reveal, shallow depth of field, warm desk lamp illumination contrasting cool blue moonlight from window.
```

### Sports intensity (push-in)
```
The camera begins a very slow push-in, moving steadily closer to the player's face. Background blurs as focus sharpens on her determined expression, ending in a tight close-up on her eyes just before she takes the shot. Cinematic sports aesthetic, tense, dramatic, aspirational, golden hour rim lighting.
```

### Emotional close-up (static + rack focus)
```
Static tripod, extreme close-up on weathered hands holding a faded photograph. Shallow depth of field, rack focus from hands to the blurred face in the background. Warm candlelight, film grain, melancholic atmosphere, shot on 35mm, slight handheld micro-movements.
```

## 2. Cyberpunk / sci-fi

### Neon alley (tracking)
```
Slow dolly push through a rain-soaked neon alley at night, holographic advertisements flicker on building walls, puddles reflect magenta and cyan light, steam rises from street grates, a lone figure in a dark coat walks away from camera. Slightly handheld with rack focus from foreground rain drops to distant figure. Blade Runner aesthetic, anamorphic lens flare, moody atmospheric fog, desaturated teal grade with crushed blacks and neon color splashes.
```

### Cyberpunk cityscape (drone)
```
FPV drone shot ascending rapidly between towering skyscrapers covered in holographic billboards, rain particles streak past camera, neon reflections shimmer on wet glass facades, camera breaks through cloud layer to reveal sprawling megacity at night. Cyberpunk, 4K, volumetric fog, electric blue and magenta palette.
```

### Tech interface (macro)
```
Extreme close-up, slow dolly in on a holographic display projecting rotating 3D data structures. Fingertips interact with floating interface elements, blue light reflects on skin. Shallow depth of field, clean futuristic aesthetic, dark environment with cool LED accents, slight lens flare on interaction points.
```

## 3. Documentary / analytical

### Clean explainer (multi-element)
```
Slow push-in across a minimal desk setup with laptop, notebook, and coffee cup. Soft natural daylight from a large window to the left, shallow depth of field, clean modern interior. Professional documentary tone, muted color palette, subtle parallax between foreground objects and background bookshelf.
```

### Archive footage feel
```
Static tripod, slightly degraded film grain, a hand carefully turns pages of an old leather-bound book on a wooden desk. Warm tungsten desk lamp creates pool of light, dust particles float in the beam. Vintage documentary aesthetic, sepia undertones, 16mm film texture.
```

### Interview context (B-roll)
```
Slow tracking shot through a modern open-plan office. On the left, a team collaborates at a whiteboard covered in diagrams. On the right, a programmer types intently at dual monitors. Soft depth of field, cool blue corporate tone, overhead LED panels, clean editorial style.
```

## 4. Tech / finance / crypto

### Data stream
```
Flowing streams of luminous blue data particles cascading through a dark void, connecting nodes of light in a vast network graph. Camera slowly pushes forward through the data stream. Holographic blue and white color palette, soft volumetric glow, sci-fi aesthetic, 4K.
```

### Trading floor
```
Cinematic slow tilt right across multiple glowing trading monitors displaying market data, green and red numbers reflecting on a dark polished desk surface. Soft ambient blue light from screens illuminating a modern office. Shallow depth of field, professional finance aesthetic, cool desaturated grade.
```

### Blockchain visualization
```
Abstract visualization of blockchain network, glowing golden nodes connected by light beams forming an expanding chain structure. Dark background with warm amber and cool blue accents. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal vast interconnected network. Volumetric lighting, futuristic tech aesthetic, clean geometric design.
```

### Server room
```
Slow dolly through rows of server racks in a dark data center. Blue and green LED indicators blink rhythmically. Cool fluorescent overhead strips create geometric light patterns on the floor. Camera passes between racks with shallow depth of field. Corporate tech aesthetic, clean, precise, slightly ominous.
```

## 5. Product showcase

### Lipstick commercial (macro)
```
Extreme close-up, macro lens. The lipstick texture slowly rotates on a reflective black surface. Water droplets slide down the smooth red surface. Soft rack focus to the brand logo in the background. High-end commercial lighting, soft diffused key light with sharp rim light, minimal clean aesthetic.
```

### Sneaker reveal (orbit)
```
Orbit shot around a floating sneaker against a clean gradient background. Camera completes a slow 180-degree arc as the shoe rotates. Dramatic studio lighting with sharp shadows, one warm key light and one cool fill. Product photography aesthetic, crisp detail, shallow depth of field on laces and texture.
```

### Food plating (overhead descent)
```
Bird's eye view slowly descending toward a perfectly plated dish on a dark slate surface. Steam rises from the food, garnish details become visible as camera approaches. Warm overhead spotlight creates defined shadows. Food photography aesthetic, rich saturated colors, extreme shallow focus at final position.
```

## 6. Abstract / conceptual

### Time passage
```
Timelapse-style shot of shadows moving across a bare white wall as sunlight shifts. A single chair sits in the center of frame. Shadows lengthen and color shifts from warm gold to cool blue. Static tripod, minimal composition, contemplative atmosphere, clean architectural interior.
```

### Chaos to order
```
Abstract particles in chaotic random motion gradually organize into geometric patterns. Camera slowly zooms out as order emerges. Dark background with electric blue and white particles. Smooth fluid motion, mathematical precision, clean minimal aesthetic, slight motion blur on fast particles.
```

### Emotional metaphor — isolation
```
Slow crane shot descending on a single small boat drifting on a vast calm ocean. Overcast grey sky merges with grey water at horizon. No other objects visible. Muted desaturated palette, atmospheric haze, melancholic, shot on long telephoto lens compressing distance.
```

## 7. Nature / environment

### Underwater
```
Slow dolly forward through crystal-clear tropical water. Sunlight beams pierce the surface creating volumetric god rays. Colorful coral formations and small fish pass on both sides. Camera gently descends deeper. Underwater documentary aesthetic, blue-green palette, natural caustic light patterns on sandy bottom.
```

### Storm approach
```
Static wide shot of a golden wheat field stretching to horizon. Dark storm clouds roll in from the right, casting a moving shadow line across the field. Wind begins bending the wheat. Dramatic contrast between golden sunlit foreground and dark sky. Epic landscape photography, deep focus, high dynamic range.
```

## Negative prompt defaults

Use in the negative prompt field (not in the main prompt):

### General purpose
```
blur, distorted hands, extra fingers, watermark, text overlay, flickering, morphing faces, unnatural physics, jittery motion, low resolution
```

### For human subjects
```
blur, distorted hands, extra fingers, asymmetric face, crossed eyes, extra limbs, morphing, flickering, unnatural skin texture
```

### For product shots
```
blur, warping, text, watermark, distortion, flickering, unnatural reflections, floating objects
```

## Image-to-Video prompt patterns

When using I2V, describe **only motion** — never redescribe the image content.

### Animate a landscape
```
Gentle wind moves through the grass, clouds drift slowly left to right, birds fly across the distant sky. Camera holds static on tripod.
```

### Animate a portrait
```
Subject slowly turns head to face camera, slight smile forms, hair moves gently as if in a breeze. Shallow depth of field maintained.
```

### Animate a product shot
```
Product slowly rotates 45 degrees clockwise, light reflections shift across surface. Camera performs gentle push-in ending 20% closer.
```

### Animate an architectural photo
```
Slow crane up revealing more of the building, clouds move in background, pedestrians walk at ground level. Traffic lights change color.
```
